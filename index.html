About

Backend that supports your product goals

I help startups and established teams ship reliable backends: from REST APIs and authentication to database design and third-party integrations. My focus is on performance, security, and maintainability so your product can grow without rewrites.

Day to day I work with Laravel and PHP, design schemas in MySQL, and connect headless stacks—such as Strapi paired with Next.js—so your frontend and content layer stay fast and flexible. Whether you need a greenfield API or help scaling an existing system, I align technical decisions with business outcomes.
