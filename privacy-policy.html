Privacy Policy

Last updated April 1, 2026

This page describes how information may be collected and used on this portfolio website. It is a starter template—have it reviewed by a qualified professional before relying on it for a production business.

Information you provide

If you use the contact form, any details you enter (such as name, email, phone, city, and message) are processed only in your browser in this demo. If you connect the form to email, a CRM, or a server, your privacy policy should explain how that service stores and uses data.

Analytics & cookies

This static template does not set analytics cookies by default. If you add analytics or advertising tools, update this section to name those providers and describe opt-out options where required.

Third-party links

This site may link to third parties (for example LinkedIn or GitHub). Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, use the contact options on the main site.
